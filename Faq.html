FAQ

how does it work?

The pizza delivery website allows customers to place their pizza orders online. Users can browse through the menu, select their desired pizza flavors and sizes, customize toppings, and add items to their cart.

how long does it take for delivery?

The delivery time for pizzas can vary depending on several factors such as the restaurant's location, current demand, traffic conditions, and the distance between the restaurant and the delivery address.

can I order for huge parties?

Yes, most pizza delivery websites offer options for large or party-sized orders. They often have special deals or packages specifically designed for catering to large groups or parties.

how much protein it contains?

The protein content of pizzas can vary depending on the crust, toppings, and cheese used. Some toppings like meat or chicken can contribute to the protein content. However, the specific protein content of a pizza will depend on the ingredients and their quantities

is it cooked with oil?

Pizza dough is typically made with ingredients that include water, flour, yeast, salt, and sometimes oil. The use of oil in the dough helps to add flavor and texture to the crust.
